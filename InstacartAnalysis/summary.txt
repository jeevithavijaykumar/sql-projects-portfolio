Project Title:
Instacart Market Basket Analysis with PostgreSQL

Project Objective:
Analyze the Instacart online grocery shopping dataset to uncover customer ordering behavior, popular products, reorder rates, and overall shopping trends by building a normalized relational database in PostgreSQL and writing advanced SQL queries for actionable business insights.

Files Included:
- instacart_market_basket_analysis.sql — SQL script to create tables and perform analysis queries
- Dataset CSV files for orders, departments, products, aisles, and order_products tables
- summary.txt — Project overview and summary

Key Insights from Analysis:
- Total orders, unique users, and products overview
- Peak order times by hour and day of week
- Top products by frequency and reorder rate
- Average basket size per order
- User loyalty and repeat purchase behavior
- Popular departments and aisles with unique products
